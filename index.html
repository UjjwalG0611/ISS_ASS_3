My Hometown - Pune

Vibrant Pune Skyline

Welcome to Pune - The Oxford of the East! A city where tradition meets innovation, hosting prestigious educational institutions and a thriving IT sector. As Maharashtra's cultural capital, Pune perfectly balances its rich historical heritage with modern development.

Cultural Capital Educational Hub IT Powerhouse

Majestic Shaniwar Wada

Step back in time at Shaniwar Wada, the grand fortified residence of the Peshwas. Built in 1732, this architectural marvel showcases the might of the Maratha Empire. Its imposing gates, intricate carvings, and legendary fountains tell stories of a glorious past.

Historical Palace Maratha Heritage Est. 1732

Historic Aga Khan Palace

A symbol of India's freedom struggle, the Aga Khan Palace stands as a testament to our nation's journey to independence. This architectural gem, built in 1892, gained prominence as the place where Mahatma Gandhi and other freedom fighters were held during the Quit India Movement.

Freedom Monument Gandhi's Legacy Heritage Site
